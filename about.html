About the Campaign

Green Meram is an initiative under the Green Champion Campaign, aimed at spreading environmental awareness across oil & gas worksites and local communities.

Our programs include weekly Green Friday Talks, awareness workshops, waste management drives, and tree-planting activities. Together, we envision a future where development and sustainability go hand in hand.
